How it Works

Create a beautiful, interactive event page for your friends in seconds.

watch the video

Plan group activities

Whether you're planning a private event or a public fundraiser, Myhub's the perfect fit. Simply enter details for what, when, and where, choose from our advanced options and you're all set.


What

Enter your title.

When

Enter your start & end time.

Where

Share the location with the drop of a pin.

Collect cash in one place

Myhub makes it incredibly easy to collect money from a group of friends. As host, you choose how you want to collect the money, how much, and you're done. We offer industry low transaction fee's with secure payment processing and next day payout to help make Myhub a win-win for everyone.


Fixed Amount

Collect a fixed amount per person.

Donations

Let friends pitch in any amount they want.

Crowdfund

Set a financial goal for your event.

Send invites

Myhub offers a built-in social network for sending quick invitations to your top friends while also reaching into your Contacts and other email accounts so you can reach all your friends, anytime.


Myhub

Build a following for faster invites.

Email

Send invites via email and Gmail.

SMS

Send invites direct from mobile.

Collaborate with everyone

Every event on Myhub comes with a built-in group chat system, smart notifications, and an activity feed so everyone can collaborate around your event and stay in the know when details change.


Messages

Chat with your group.

Activity

Keep track of what's happening.

Notifications

Receive updates on the go.

Manage everything right here

As host, you have complete control at all times, meaning you can edit any detail up until the event starts. As guest, we've incorporated a familiar RSVP platform that'll keep you updated with upcoming plans.

Join

You're on the guestlist & will recieve notifications.

Maybe

We'll save this event & sign you up for notifications.

Decline

We'll notify your host and remove this event from your feed.

Available on iOS, Desktop, and Mobile Web

Myhub is currently available as a native iOS app with a Web platform available and more features on the way. Join us and plan your next adventure.
